About

Welcome to our website and our game "DESTINY."
Our purpose in creating this game is to show people who are not aware of bullying, suicide, and distracted driving that it is happening now and should not be belittled. Depending on what you choose, you will experience the harsh life of a teenage girl who goes through hardships. Below is the game you will play in order to do so.














    Your alarm just rang, do you want to snooze your alarm or just get up?

    Do you want to text back now or text back later?
